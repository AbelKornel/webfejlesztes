Official DayZ Maps

Chernarus is the primary map featured in the popular survival game DayZ. It's a fictional post-Soviet country located in Eastern Europe, known for its diverse landscapes, rolling hills, cities and coastal areas. The map is vast and offers a mix of rural and urban environments, providing players with various challenges and opportunities for survival. Players navigate through abandoned towns, military bases, farmlands, and wilderness while scavenging for resources, avoiding zombies, and interacting with other players in their quest to survive the harsh and unpredictable world of DayZ.

Livonia, an Eastern European DLC map in DayZ, boasts a wild and untamed landscape characterized by its dense forests, sprawling meadows, winding rivers, and mist-covered mountains. Players traverse through a mosaic of environments, from secluded woodland cabins to overgrown military encampments, amidst the backdrop of a post-apocalyptic world. Survivors navigate through the region's contrasting terrains, scavenging for resources in forgotten villages, dodging undead threats, and contending with the unpredictable elements that define this hauntingly beautiful yet perilous setting.

Player Made Modded Maps

Namalsk, another player-created modded map in DayZ, introduces a frigid and unforgiving environment characterized by icy tundras, snow-covered landscapes, and harsh weather conditions. While not an official map, it immerses players in a chilling atmosphere where survival becomes a constant struggle against the elements and undead threats. The map's unique setting includes hidden underground facilities, frozen military installations, and isolated civilian outposts, offering a distinct and challenging survival experience. Despite potential imperfections, Namalsk captivates players with its eerie beauty and the relentless test of survival it presents in this frozen wasteland.

Deer Isle is a player-made modded map in DayZ. While not an official map, it provides an immersive environment with scattered towns, hidden bunkers, and unique landmarks. Survivors navigate through its diverse terrains, exploring abandoned settlements, uncovering remnants of civilization, and confronting the challenges of an altered world. Although not flawless, the map's detailed design captures the essence of survival in a post-apocalyptic setting, making it an engaging playground for players seeking new adventures.
